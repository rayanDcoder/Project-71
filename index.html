What's New?

  • New School Canteen

    The food options have become more varied, so the kids can eat what they want, but the food served has become healthier and will only serve meals with high protein, carbs, etc. The cooks who will make the food wil also be pre-trained to be able to make tasty, but also healthy dishes.

    The school has implemented a new system where you wil be notified if your ward has reached the school campus safely, and at what time. This is to help us manage our record of absentees and inform you that your ward is safe.
